# Code of conduct

## Our standard

AxiomaticGW welcomes constructive participation from people of every
background and level of Lean experience. Contributors are expected to:

- discuss mathematical and technical disagreements respectfully;
- give actionable, evidence-based review feedback;
- acknowledge uncertainty and correct mistakes openly;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and unwanted
  disclosure of private information;
- respect the scope and conventions recorded in the project notes.

## Enforcement

Behavior that violates this standard may be edited, removed, or rejected, and
participants may be temporarily or permanently excluded from project spaces.
Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through their
GitHub profiles. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This code applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews,
and other spaces where someone is representing the project.
